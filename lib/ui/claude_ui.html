// Session Notes
Sunday Lower follows Friday Upper with a full rest day gap. You should be fully recovered — push intensity on leg press and RDLs. Since your gym is closed Saturday, this is your first session of the new week. Treat it as a fresh start; aim for progressive overload on at least one main lift. Legs need 2–3 min rest between heavy sets — don't rush.
